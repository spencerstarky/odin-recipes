Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish that traditionally consists of wide, flat lasagna noodles alternated with various fillings and sauces. The base layers often start with a rich, savory tomato-based meat sauce, commonly made with ground beef, onions, garlic, tomatoes, and herbs such as basil, oregano, and thyme. Each layer of noodles is separated by this flavorful sauce, providing a balance of texture and taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs
  • Butter, flour milk
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare Sauces: Make a meat and béchamel sauce.
  2. Mix Ricotta: Combine ricotta cheese with beaten eggs and seasonings.
  3. Cook Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  4. Layer Ingredients: In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and béchamel sauce.
  5. Bake: Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for 15 minutes until bubbly.

Slice and serve your delicious lasagna!

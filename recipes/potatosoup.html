Loaded Baked Potato Soup

Description

A loaded baked potato begins with a perfectly baked russet potato, which has a crispy skin on the outside and a fluffy, tender interior. The potato is typically baked in the oven until the skin becomes golden and the inside is soft and pillowy. Once baked, the potato is sliced open, creating a pocket ready to be filled with a variety of delicious toppings. The "loaded" aspect refers to the generous and customizable assortment of ingredients that adorn the potato, creating a flavorful and satisfying dish.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or sausage
  • Potatoes
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Salt, pepper, and herbs

Steps

  1. Sauté Onion: In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add Potatoes: Add the diced potatoes to the pot and stir to combine with the onions.
  3. Pour Broth: Pour the chicken or vegetable broth into the pot, ensuring that the potatoes are well-covered.
  4. Simmer: Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the potatoes are tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  5. Season and Serve: Once the potatoes are cooked, season the soup with salt and pepper to taste. You can also use a potato masher or immersion blender to partially blend the soup for a creamier texture, if desired. Serve hot and enjoy!

Plate and serve your delicious loaded baked potato soup!
